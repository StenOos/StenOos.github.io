About Us

In the heart of a bustling city, where craftsmanship meets creativity, Tijn's Rings was born. Our journey began in a small workshop where the beauty of rings captivated us. We, Sten Oosterwaal and Tijn Oosterwaal, saw the potential to bring unique and exquisite rings to life.

We realized that many ring enthusiasts faced a common problem: the lack of unique, high-quality rings that could stand out in a crowd. This gap in the market inspired us to take action and create something exceptional.

With a vision in mind, we embarked on our journey. Starting our own business was no small feat. From sourcing the finest materials to mastering intricate designs, we encountered numerous obstacles. But our passion for creating the perfect ring kept us going.

Today, Tijn's Rings is proud to offer a wide range of rings that blend quality with uniqueness. We have reached significant milestones, including the launch of our signature collections and gaining a loyal customer base. Each ring is crafted with precision and love, ensuring it meets our high standards.

Looking ahead, we aim to expand our horizons and continue innovating in the world of jewelry. Our mission is to create rings that not only adorn but also tell a story. Join us as we embark on this exciting journey and discover the future of Tijn's Rings.
